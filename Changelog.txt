## [0.0.1] - 2018-03-26
### Added
- Forms for t cards.
- MongoDB integration with forms (messy and temporary).
- Cards can be added to "On Call" board (and nothing else as of now).
- Cards can be deleted.
- Names are added to cards.
- Cards are draggable between each board.
- Materialize should now be included when cloning the repo.

### Bugs
